Should your firm move ediscovery in-house or does managed ediscovery make more sense? What variables should be considered when taking on this challenge?

eDiscovery Here to Stay

eDiscovery isn't going anywhere and as many law firms attempt to drive down costs by bringing the process in-house the price tag to properly setup infrastructure, training and management can quickly become overwhelming. Not to mention the need to continue to processing and evolving with the industry for years to come.

Soutions

Orange Legal Technologies has as solution to fit any orginizations needs. OLT's solutions can range anywhere from direct to complex. Check out our Solutions menu for a list of services we typically recommend.

Small Firms

Small and large organizations can benefit setting up a government-approved secure remote connection in a matter of minutes which will allow a workflow beginning with firm submitted projects & instructions and ending with projects completely loaded into your system ready for review. Attorneys and paralegals will be able to view, search and review documents the moment the project is completed.

Large Firms

For large organizations who have the means to implement an in-house solution, the benefits of partnering with a vendor who has their finger on the pulse of industry changes and a team with decades of experience is more crucial than ever. OLT can manage your processing or review platform remotely or place a qualified team member in your firm who will have complete access to the entire OLT knowledge base, experience, and software.
